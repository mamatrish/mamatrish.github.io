Welcome to Wrap it Up!

Craving something fresh, flavorful, and entirely plant-based? Say hello to Wrap It Up!, Chicago’s premier vegan food truck! We’re serving up wraps that are as bold and vibrant as the city itself, crafted with love and packed with the freshest ingredients. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan, curious foodie, or just in need of a quick and satisfying meal, Wrap It Up! has something for everyone.

As a proud Chicago-based food truck, we’re all about bringing plant-powered goodness to every corner of the Windy City. From the bustling downtown streets to community parks, farmer’s markets, and festivals, we’re always on the move, sharing our passion for wholesome, flavorful food. Each week, we roll into new locations, so you’re never far from your next favorite meal.

At Wrap it up, we're all about bringing healthy, soul-nourishing eats right to your parking lot or doorstep! Our food truck cruises all over Chicagoland, sometimes picking the spot of the day, and other times arriving at your special request. Whether it's your office, a corporate shindig, private party, or a lively block party, we’re on the move to serve you. Our wraps, packed with 100% whole foods, are crafted to keep both vegans and non-vegans energized and happy. We want you to get excited about nourishing your body when we roll into your neck of the woods. Get ready to wrap up your hunger with something delicious!
