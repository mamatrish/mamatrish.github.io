WHAT IS CHAI TEA?

Chai is a blend of black tea, spices, and herbs. It is often served with milk and sweetened with sugar or honey. Chai is a spiced tea derived from the Assam region of India. Over the past 10 years, it's grown in popularity around the globe. Chai is believed to have numerous benefits such as aiding digestion, boosting the immune system, and improving heart health.
